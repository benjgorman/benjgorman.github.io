I support teams in understanding users, validating assumptions, and improving digital experiences through research-driven insight and practical guidance. My role is to help you make products more usable, more accessible, and more aligned with how people actually behave — without requiring a full-time researcher.

What I Offer

  • UX Research Support
    Planning studies, refining research questions, evaluating usability, running or reviewing user tests.
  • Accessibility Guidance
    WCAG-aware assessments, inclusive design feedback, assistive tech considerations built into design decisions.
  • Behavioural Insight
    Making sense of friction, motivation, drop-off and user behaviour to guide product direction.
  • Product Discovery & Insight
    Identifying user needs, testing assumptions, framing opportunities with evidence.
  • Prototype Consultation
    Technical support and prototyping guidance (React, Node/Express, React Native) to test and validate ideas quickly.
  • Rapid UX Review
    Heuristic evaluation with prioritised, actionable recommendations — fast turnaround available.
  • GenAI UX Input
    Reviewing AI-driven features, user experience risks, interaction behaviours and safe integration patterns.

Engagement Models

  • Prototype & Feature Guidance

Who This Helps

Early-stage teams validating product direction. Product teams who need clarity behind decisions. Organisations improving accessibility or UX maturity. If you want research that leads to decisions — not documents — this is for you.
